Magic Eyes of Destruction

These Magic Eyes are capable of destroying everything in the user's sight. They are capable of destroying a person's mind and nullify magic spells. Additionally, these Magic Eyes can put people to sleep for a day or even a year.

Jio Graze

The highest grade of fire magic within the demons' magic system; a powerful miniature jet-black sun which can be fired off at the enemy. The projectile is fired using a Hellfire Mortar created by the combined magic of multiple users, with complete control given to one person. However, Anos can single-handedly cast the spell, even without the use of the mortar itself.

Vebdoz

A spell that allows the caster to directly touch someone's source, allowing them to manipulate, injure, and destroy it. Just a small scratch from Vebdoz is extremely painful. A pain that surpasses all the pain in the world combined is felt when the source is hurt. When the source is hurt, the deaths of all of one's future incarnations occur at once.

Beno Ievun

The wall that separated the four races two thousand years ago. It was made by combining the magic power of the Goddess of Creation, the Great Spirit, the Hero, and the Demon King. It takes the form of a black aurora that glows ominously and repels everything with destruction, including all magic cast at it.

Jirasd

Rivide

Origin magic that allows the caster to manipulate the local time of objects and spells. If you try to tamper with the past itself, not locally, you will be confronted by Eugo La Raviaz, the Keeper of Time, who watches over the Order of Time.
